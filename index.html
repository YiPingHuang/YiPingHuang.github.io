Welcome!

    I got my Ph. D. at CU Boulder working in condensed matter theory with Prof. Michael Hermele. I am now a postdoctoral scholar at Max Plank Institute for Physics of Complex Systems.

    My research interests lie in the field of theoretical condensed matter physics including strongly correlated systems, quantum magnetism, ultracold atoms and topological phases protected/enriched by symmetries.

    Currently, I am interested in the physics of quantum phases of matter. Especially, I am interested in finding realistic models which support new entangled quantum phases and the corresponding smoking gun experimental/numerical signatures. Also, I am branching out to dynamics in correlated systems.

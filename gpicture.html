Description of my interests for general reader

My research interests focusing on the exotic emergent phenomena due to composite effects of fundamental physics. The competition between different effects made those system interesting in various aspects(So my interest in physics diverges in some way...).

To me, I would like to know the general properties of those effects. What is its nature? For example, how the long-ranged anisotropic dipole-dipole interaction affect the quasi-one-dimensional physics? Understanding the interplay between space anisotropy and anisotropy of interaction is the physics that I enjoy a lot. How large spin-orbit coupling changes our understanding about systems without large spin-orbit coupling? Finding spin-orbit coupling provides a chance to realize more constrained model under space group symmetry and further helps realizing the exotic topological insulator and topological order is exciting!

In addition to theory oriented motivations, I also like to understand what happened in physical reality. I would like to develop the ability to identify interesting physics also from experimental side.

Super short CV

I am interested in theoretical condensed matter physics. I am currently focusing on understanding quantum emergent phenomena due to composite effects that could lead to exotic orders in nature. These effects could came from a wide range of systems. They could came from strong spin-orbit coupling system with different lattice structure in solid state materials such as transition metal oxides and rare-earth oxides or from exotic emergent interactions in ultracold atoms.
